Our Story

Our Company Milestones

2015 - A Legacy of Innovation

Early Years: In 2015, GoCloud emerged with a clear vision to revolutionize the technology landscape in Egypt. Starting with a dedicated team of innovative professionals, we recognized the growing demand for comprehensive digital solutions in the MENA region. Our initial focus was on delivering customized software solutions that would help local businesses embrace digital transformation and compete in an increasingly technology-driven market.

2017 - More Than One Service

Expansion & Innovation: As technology continued to evolve, so we expanded our service offerings to include a wide range of solutions, from IT services and cloud computing to data analytics and artificial intelligence.

2023 - ELITE Boom

Medical insurance solution: ELITE is our comprehensive medical insurance management system, designed specifically for the healthcare insurance industry. This innovative solution streamlines the entire insurance lifecycle - from policy creation and claims processing to provider network management and regulatory compliance. Built with cutting-edge technology, ELITE empowers insurance companies to deliver efficient, transparent, and customer-centric services while optimizing operational costs.

2025 - ERP & CRM Era

Odoo ERP & CRM Solutions: We specialize in implementing comprehensive Odoo ERP & CRM systems tailored for diverse business sectors. Our solutions empower real estate companies with property management and sales tracking, help gold investment firms streamline operations and inventory control, and enable medical facilities to efficiently manage patient records and healthcare services - all through one integrated platform.
